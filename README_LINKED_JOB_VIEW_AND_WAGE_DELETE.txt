PB JOBHUB - LINKED JOB VIEW + WAGE DELETE

Added:
- New menu item: Job Lookup / Links
- Select by job number, job name, builder/client, address, status or leading hand.
- Opening a job shows linked:
  - job details
  - builder/client details
  - materials
  - imported PDF checklist materials
  - wage entries
  - timesheets
  - equipment
  - estimate working sheets
  - variations
  - invoice/claim records
  - staff schedule
  - photos
- Jobs page now has an Open Linked Job Info section in the Job Register.
- Search by Builder can now jump directly to the builder/client in Job Lookup.
- Builders & Clients list now shows all jobs linked to the selected builder/client.
- Wages page now includes Delete Wage Entries with confirmation.

How to use:
1. Open Job Lookup / Links.
2. Choose Open Job, Jobs by Builder / Client, Jobs by Leading Hand, or Search Anything.
3. Select the job to open the full linked file.
4. For wages, open Wages, select the wage entry/entries, type DELETE WAGES, and click Delete Selected Wage Entries.

This update does not delete existing data.
